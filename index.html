#100 Traveling Puzzles Project

Traveling puzzles are my curious reaction to wanting to enjoy putting together a good puzzle, but at the same time trying to minimize the objects that take residence in my life. Sharing puzzles with my friends and family is a great way to build community while concentrating on the things that really matter -- not the stuff in our lives, but the experiences that we choose to share with each other!
